About Us

Mission Statement

Our mission is to hand-select the highest quality vinyl for the neighborhood and provide a streamlined, informative experience for newcomers and seasoned diggers alike.  We go out of our way to source timeless albums to keep our stock eclectic and fresh. We are driven by a passion for records, and strive to share our love with every customer through great values and fair trades.

Business History/Backstory

Paw Paw Records was founded by Ryan Outcault and Ezra Horne in 2015 as a pop-up shop in their Silver Lake driveway. In April 2016, they expanded to a brick-and-mortar location in the heart of Virgil Village, just steps from their apartment.  The name for the store was derives from the digging that dogs do, and how Ryan and Ezra get their paws dirty on your behalf, finding the treasure and bringing it into a clean, organized retail environment.  Paw Paw is the name of a fictional pet in Miranda July’s "The Future", and we felt the name suited our adorable pup mascot.
