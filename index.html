Speaking of wild sides, I love boba (if you have not noticed already)!! I spend a good amount of my time (and expenses) on these sugary drinks with tapioca balls inside of them. It is commonly known as boba drinks, tapioca drinks, and milk tea with boba/pearls. However, I have to admit that I don't get the boba/pearls/tapioca with my drink most of the time. But I enjoy the drink itself! I probably used to get at least one or two on a daily basis. Thankfully, I have stopped myself from consuming so much boba lately.


Aside from having a bit of a sugar addiction problem, I spend some of my free time on Summoner's Rift. For those of you who don't know, Summoner's Rift is a map/gamemode for League of Legends. Competitively, I am currently ranked as Gold I. However, I wouldn't be surprised if I played like a lower ranked because I am not super serious about the game. I would say that I spend a lot more time playing casually with friends than playing for rank.
