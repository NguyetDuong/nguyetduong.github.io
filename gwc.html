Office Hours


So what are office hours? If you don't know, they are times allocated outside of the classroom environment for you to ask me any questions related to the class. This can be for extra help on certain topics/materials or questions about computer science in general.


My office hours are Tuesdays and Thursdays at 8pm to 9pm. It will all be online. When you are ready or have a question, join the call.
